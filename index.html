Course Overview

Course Purpose

This first-of-its-kind course at Ivey fills a significant gap by exploring the fast-growing, high-stakes world of sports marketing business through exclusive access to top-tier industry executives.

Format

  • Duration: 5 weeks, 30-40 hours total
  • Structure: Live sessions + pre-readings + executive Q&A
  • Enrollment: 50 students maximum
  • Delivery: Blended learning approach

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

1

Analyze Revenue Drivers

Understand and evaluate the core revenue drivers and marketing strategies that power the sports industry

2

Evaluate Business Models

Critically analyze business models behind media rights, live content, and fan engagement strategies

3

Assess Sponsorship Programs

Evaluate the structure and effectiveness of sports sponsorship programs using industry frameworks

4

Explore Women's Sports Growth

Analyze the explosive growth of women's sports and related brand investment opportunities

5

Strategic Athlete Branding

Gain strategic insights into athlete branding and endorsement negotiation processes

6

Assess Emerging Trends

Develop critical frameworks to assess emerging trends like sports betting and experiential technology

Assessment & Projects

Group Marketing Project

Develop a comprehensive marketing plan or campaign for a sport property, team, or event. Choose from multiple presentation formats to demonstrate your strategic thinking.

  • Strategic analysis and market research
  • Campaign development and execution plan
  • Multiple presentation options (written, visual, video, interactive)
  • Executive panel review and feedback

Executive Case Studies

Engage with real-world case studies presented by industry leaders, with multiple ways to demonstrate understanding and application.

  • Interactive discussions and Q&A sessions

  • Peer collaboration and knowledge sharing
  • Reflection and application exercises
