The Business of Marketing in Sport

Course Development & Implementation Guide

Development Storyboard

Course Learning Outcomes (CLO)

Six measurable outcomes that students will achieve through module-specific activities:

CLO 1: Analyze Revenue Drivers

Analyze the core revenue drivers and marketing strategies that power the sports industry

CLO 2: Evaluate Business Models

Evaluate business models behind media rights, live content, and fan engagement strategies

CLO 3: Assess Sponsorship Programs

Assess the structure and effectiveness of sports sponsorship programs using industry frameworks

CLO 4: Compare Women's Sports Growth

Compare the growth of women's sports with traditional marketing approaches and investment opportunities

CLO 5: Design Athlete Branding Strategies

Design comprehensive athlete branding and endorsement strategies using industry frameworks

CLO 6: Develop Assessment Frameworks

Develop frameworks to assess emerging trends like sports betting and experiential technology

Assessment & Evaluation Framework

Group Marketing Project (60%)

All CLOs
Comprehensive strategic marketing plan development

Project Components:

  • Industry analysis and market research (CLO 1, 2, 6)
  • Strategic positioning and target audience (CLO 3, 4, 5)
  • Campaign development and execution plan (CLO 1, 3, 6)
  • Executive presentation and defense (All CLOs)

UDL Expression Options:

  • Written strategic plan with visual components
  • Video presentation with supporting materials
  • Interactive digital campaign proposal
  • Live presentation with Q&A session

Module-Specific
Active participation and application across all modules

Engagement Components:

  • Pre-session preparation and research (20%)

  • Post-session reflection and application (30%)

Multiple Assessment Methods:

  • Written case analysis submissions

  • Creative application projects
