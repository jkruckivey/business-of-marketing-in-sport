ü§ñ AI-Enhanced Learning (PAIRR Methodology)

Every major assessment uses Peer and AI Review + Reflection (PAIRR) to develop AI literacy alongside sports business expertise. This is a signature pedagogical innovation‚Äînot just "use ChatGPT," but compare human vs. AI feedback quality.

PAIRR 5-Phase Process:

Phase 1: Submit 80% draft by Day 5
Phase 2: Receive BOTH peer + ChatGPT feedback by Day 7
Phase 3: Comparative Reflection (300-400 words): Which was more useful? Why?
Phase 4: Revise using integrated insights, submit final version Day 10
Phase 5: Post-Revision Reflection (200-300 words): How did feedback change your thinking?

AI Roleplay Scenarios (Diagnostic Practice):

Example: "The $100M Question"

You're presenting to the GM who just signed Connor McDavid to $100M over 8 years.
The CFO shows: $12.5M cost - $8M revenue = -$4.5M loss per year.

Challenge: Defend the deal to a skeptical Board. Where's the missing $22-32M in value?

AI Executive Response: "Your CFO is measuring the wrong things. Walk me through your revenue ecosystem analysis. I want to see media leverage, sponsorship multipliers, and betting handle impact‚Äîwith numbers I can defend to our owners."

Marketing Angle: "Not just another course that says 'use AI'‚Äîwe train students to evaluate AI quality through systematic peer comparison. That's the literacy employers actually need."
