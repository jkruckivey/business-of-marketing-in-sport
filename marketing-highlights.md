# Executive Interview Highlights for Marketing

## Course Overview
Five exclusive executive interviews featuring senior leaders from the NY Giants, Rogers Sports Media, FanDuel, Fairgrounds, and Golf Canada. Over 195 minutes of insider perspectives on sports business, media rights, betting marketing, emerging sports, and digital transformation.

---

## Week 1: Kevin Abrams
**SVP Football Operations & Strategy, New York Giants**

### Quotable Soundbites
> "We're a **$10 billion mom and pop shop**"

> "We used to treat the salary cap like a **checking account**, now we understand it's a **portfolio**"

> "The **quality of the prompt dictates the quality of the output**" — on AI implementation

> "Players are coming out of college now where **they are brands**" — on the NIL era

> "I had to do **6 or 7 internships** before I got my first full-time role"

### Key Stats
- 25+ years with NY Giants organization
- $10B franchise valuation (most valuable in NFL)
- Enterprise AI (ChatGPT) deployed across talent evaluation, HR, and operations

## Week 2: Al Dak
**SVP Revenue, Rogers Sports Media**

### Quotable Soundbites
> "Sports **swims upstream** against fragmentation"

> "There isn't a **bigger owner of sports rights and teams in North America** now than Rogers"

> "We manage inventory **like airlines do today**" — on dynamic pricing

> "Sports is the **true last mass medium**"

> "**Don't be afraid to ask for help** — most people will take the time to sit down with you"

### Key Stats

- Sportsnet revenue grew **6x** (from high $90M to $600M+)
- Cable penetration still **low 70%** in Canada (vs. below 50% in US)
- Brand health studies for **90% of sponsors** annually

## Week 3: Andrew Sneyd
**Chief Marketing Officer, FanDuel**

### Quotable Soundbites
> "**Trust, Easy, Fun**" — the three brand pillars

> "The risk was in **no one paying attention** — we certainly had everyone's attention"

> "Marketing is **equal parts science and art**... the art is still 33%"

> "**Be curious** — those little earworms make such a difference"

> "You can only measure what's happened in the past"

### Key Stats
- Brand awareness jumped **40% to 50%** from Kick of Destiny campaign
- **$1 billion** spent on TV/media — all paying back within 18 months
- **1 billion emails** sent per year
- **13 million people** signed up around Super Bowl
- **10% of all Ohio adults** signed up for FanDuel in first 30 days
- **80%** of sports bets are player props (not game outcomes)
- NFL ratings were declining until sports betting legalized

## Week 4: Matt Rubinoff
**Co-Founder, Fairgrounds**

### Quotable Soundbites

> "**Pickleball is NASCAR, paddle is F1**" — on market positioning
