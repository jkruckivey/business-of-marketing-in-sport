Module Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

1

Map Major Revenue Streams

Map the major revenue streams in sport: media rights, sponsorship, ticketing, merchandising, betting, licensing (CLO 1)

2

Understand Sports Business Model

Understand how sport's business model differs from traditional consumer industries (CLO 1)

3

Analyze Revenue Vulnerability

Debate which revenue streams are most vulnerable and which hold the most growth potential (CLO 1)

4

Evaluate Revenue Sharing

Analyze revenue sharing models and their impact on competitive balance using NHL Vancouver Canucks case (CLO 1)

Executive Leadership

Bell Media / TSN Senior Leader

This module features insights from a senior executive at Bell Media, Canada's leading sports media company. Our guest leader will share real-world experience in:

  • Revenue Stream Mapping: Understanding the six major revenue sources in sports business
  • Media Rights Strategy: How TSN evaluates and negotiates for sports properties
  • Business Model Innovation: What makes sports different from traditional industries
  • Revenue Vulnerability: Identifying risks and growth opportunities across revenue streams
  • Revenue Sharing: Lessons from professional leagues and competitive balance
Accessibility Note: All video content includes captions and full transcripts. Materials are provided in multiple formats (audio, visual, text) to support diverse learning preferences.
