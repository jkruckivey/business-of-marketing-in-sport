Learning Outcomes

MLO 1.1: Map Major Revenue Streams

  • Pre-session: Revenue stream mapping exercise (individual, 30 min)

Week 1 Assessment Summary

  • Revenue stream mapping exercise (MLO 1.1)
  • Business model comparison table (MLO 1.2)
  • Risk/opportunity matrix (MLO 1.3)
  • Case study prep questions (MLO 1.4)

Active Learning (Executive Session)

  • Revenue stream vulnerability discussion (MLO 1.1, 1.3)
  • Live Q&A on industry differences (MLO 1.2)

Executive Session Structure (90 minutes)

Part 1: TSN Revenue Strategy (30 min)

  • Bell Media/TSN executive presentation on revenue streams
  • How TSN evaluates and negotiates for sports properties
  • Q&A on media rights and monetization

Part 2: Interactive Discussion (40 min)

  • Small group breakouts: Revenue vulnerability debate (20 min)
  • Full group discussion with executive feedback (20 min)

Part 3: Case Application (20 min)

  • NHL Canucks case discussion with TSN perspective
  • How revenue sharing affects media rights negotiations
  • Executive insights on competitive balance and league strategy
