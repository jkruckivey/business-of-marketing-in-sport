Week 2: Media, Content & Fan Engagement (The Core Product)

Executive Leader: TSN or DAZN Canada Leader

Core Case: Rogers Communications: Sportsnet and NHL Rights Deal (HBS W16179)

Industry Focus: Media rights valuation, streaming vs traditional broadcasting, fan monetization

Learning Outcomes

MLO 2.1: Explore Media Rights Valuation

Aligns with CLO 2: Evaluate Media & Fan Monetization

Outcome: Explore how media rights deals are valued and monetized

Supporting Content:

  • Rogers Communications case - $5.2B NHL deal analysis
  • Reading: "Why Sports Rights Are the Last Must-Have for TV" (HBR 2021)
  • Video: "Media Rights Economics 101" (15 min)
  • Interactive Widget: Media Rights Calculator

Aligned Assessment:

  • Pre-session: Media rights valuation framework exercise (individual, 45 min)
  • Executive session: How TSN/DAZN values sports properties (group discussion)
  • Post-session: 2-page mini-case memo on Rogers deal success/failure

MLO 2.2: Compare Broadcasting Models

Aligns with CLO 2: Evaluate Media & Fan Monetization

Outcome: Compare linear broadcasting economics to streaming/direct-to-consumer

Supporting Content:

  • Rogers case - traditional TV vs streaming decision points
  • Reading: "The Streaming Revolution in Sports" (industry report, 2023)
  • Video: "DTC vs Linear: The Trade-offs" (12 min)
  • TSN/DAZN executive insights on platform strategy

Aligned Assessment:

  • Pre-session: Economic model comparison - linear vs DTC (spreadsheet analysis)
  • Executive session: Interactive Widget - Streaming Wars Strategy simulation
  • Post-session: Included in memo - platform recommendation with rationale

MLO 2.3: Understand Fan Engagement Monetization

Aligns with CLO 2: Evaluate Media & Fan Monetization

Outcome: Understand how fan engagement extends monetization through ads, subscriptions, and sponsorship activation

Supporting Content:

  • Rogers case - fan engagement strategy and digital integration
  • Reading: "From Eyeballs to Engagement" (sports media trends article)
  • Interactive Widget: Fan Engagement Lab
  • Executive session: How DAZN/TSN activates sponsors through fan data

Aligned Assessment:

  • Pre-session: Fan engagement value chain diagram
  • Executive session: Group brainstorm - innovative fan monetization tactics
  • Post-session: Memo includes engagement strategy for Rogers/Sportsnet

MLO 2.4: Evaluate Rogers NHL Deal

Aligns with CLO 2: Evaluate Media & Fan Monetization

Outcome: Evaluate the Rogers $5.2B NHL deal and its industry implications

Supporting Content:

  • Rogers case - full case analysis with financial modeling
  • Supplemental: Post-deal performance data (2014-2024)
  • Video: "Rogers NHL Deal: 10 Years Later" (14 min)
  • Executive session: Industry perspective on mega-deals

Aligned Assessment:

  • Pre-session: Case study prep questions (6 questions, individual)
  • Executive session: Case discussion with TSN/DAZN executive (60 min)
  • Post-session: 2-page mini-case memo - was the deal worth it?

Executive Session Structure (90 minutes)

Part 1: TSN/DAZN Media Strategy (30 min)

  • Executive presentation on media rights evaluation and acquisition
  • Linear vs streaming platform decisions
  • Q&A on valuation and risk management

Part 2: Interactive Simulation (40 min)

  • Streaming Wars Strategy widget (small groups, 25 min)
  • Debrief with executive feedback on decisions (15 min)

Part 3: Case Application (20 min)

  • Rogers NHL deal discussion with industry perspective
  • What would TSN/DAZN have done differently?
  • Future of sports media rights in Canada
