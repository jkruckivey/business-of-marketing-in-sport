Anchor Project: Brand Strategy & Market Positioning

Milestone: Brand Strategy Brief - Target audience, positioning, and athlete/talent partnerships

Time Investment: 30 minutes during live session + 3-4 hours async team work

Faculty Review: Draft outline review provided before Week 5

Activities During Executive Session (30 min)

  • Target Audience Workshop (15 min): Define who you're reaching, demographic and psychographic profiles, why this moment matters
  • Competitive Landscape Mapping (15 min): Identify 3-5 competitors or comparable properties, map your differentiation

Post-Session Deliverable (Due by end of Week 4)

  • 2-3 Page Brand Strategy Brief:
    • Target audience persona (demographics, psychographics, behaviors)
    • Positioning statement (For [target], [property] is the [category] that [unique benefit] because [reason to believe])
    • Athlete/talent partnership recommendations (if applicable - apply Week 4 learning on endorsements)
    • Competitive analysis map showing where you sit vs. competitors

Faculty Feedback Process

  • Submit draft outline (1 page) by mid-week for faculty review
  • Faculty provides written feedback on positioning clarity and strategic fit
  • Teams revise before final submission

Resources Provided

  • Brand strategy brief template
  • Positioning statement formula and examples
  • Competitive analysis framework (perceptual map)
  • Target audience persona worksheet
