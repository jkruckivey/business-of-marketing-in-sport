# Expansion Day: A CFL Graphic Novel Script
**Style:** Retro Sports Illustrated (1970s-80s painted sports journalism aesthetic)
**Purpose:** Teach sports business concepts through visual storytelling (ties to CFL Anchor Project)

---

[COVER PAGE]
Title: EXPANSION DAY
Visual Description: A dramatic painted illustration in warm oranges, deep greens, and golden yellows. A brand-new CFL football soars through the air against a sunset sky over the Halifax harbour. In the foreground, a diverse crowd of fans in brand-new Atlantic Schooners jerseys (teal and navy) raises their arms in celebration. The Halifax Citadel looms on the hill behind a gleaming new stadium. A determined female team president in a sharp blazer stands at center, arms crossed confidently, with the Schooners logo on a massive banner unfurling behind her. Classic Sports Illustrated painted style with visible brushstrokes and warm nostalgic lighting. 16:9 aspect ratio.

## Production Notes

**Color Palette (Retro Sports Illustrated):**
- Primary: Warm oranges, golden yellows, burnt sienna
- Secondary: Teal (Schooners), deep navy, forest green
- Accents: Cream, warm grey, sunset pinks
- Shadows: Warm browns, never pure black

**Texture Guidelines:**
- Visible brushstroke texture throughout
- Subtle halftone dots in shadow areas
- Soft edges on backgrounds, crisp on focal points
- Nostalgic grain overlay (subtle)

**Typography Suggestions:**
- Title: Bold serif, slightly distressed (1970s sports magazine)
- Captions: Clean serif, italic for emphasis
- Dialogue: Hand-lettered style, bold for emphasis
- SFX: Dynamic, expressive lettering with motion

**Course Concepts Illustrated:**
- Page 2-4: Ownership structure, league governance, expansion voting
- Page 6: Stadium financing, sponsorship acquisition, media relations
- Page 7: Community engagement, fan development, brand building
- Page 8: Financial challenges, cost overruns, stakeholder management
- Page 9-10: Media rights payoff, franchise launch, long-term vision

**Connection to Anchor Project:**
Students can use this narrative as inspiration for their own CFL expansion city. The story demonstrates the business challenges they'll address in their proposals: market selection, stadium financing, media rights, sponsorship, community building.
