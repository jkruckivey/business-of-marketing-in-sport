# Five Streams: A Nashville Predators Graphic Novel Script
**Week 1: The Business of Sport - Where the Money Flows**
**Style:** Retro Sports Illustrated - 1970s-80s painted sports magazine look with warm colors and visible brushstrokes
**Purpose:** Introduce the 5 revenue streams of professional sports through visual storytelling

---

[COVER PAGE]
Title: FIVE STREAMS
Visual Description: Painted illustration of Bridgestone Arena in Nashville at night glowing gold. Five golden streams of light flow into the arena from different directions. Each stream has a clear label floating above it: "TICKETS" from the left, "MEDIA" from the top, "SPONSORS" from the right, "MERCHANDISE" from the bottom-left, "PREMIUM" from the bottom-right. A confident Latina woman in her 40s (JESSICA TORRES) stands in the foreground wearing a navy blazer, arms crossed. The Nashville skyline frames the scene. Warm gold and amber colors throughout. 16:9 aspect ratio.

[PAGE 3]
Layout Note: Top wide panel, bottom two equal panels.
Style Note: Warmer tones as hope enters.

[PANEL 1]
Shot Type & Angle: Medium shot at airport.
Visual Description: Nashville Airport. JESSICA TORRES walks through the terminal with a carry-on, wearing a leather jacket over a Predators t-shirt. She's Latina, mid-40s, with sharp eyes and confident posture. She pauses at a window looking out at the city.
Caption: Jessica Torres had left Nashville at 18 with nothing. She'd worked her way from intern to VP at Maple Leaf Sports & Entertainment.
Dialogue (JESSICA): "Home. Let's see if you remember me."

[PANEL 2]
Shot Type & Angle: Wide establishing shot.
Visual Description: Jessica stands outside Bridgestone Arena the next morning, coffee in hand, studying the building. She holds a notebook, already sketching ideas.
Caption: Day One: Assessment.
Dialogue (JESSICA): "Five revenue streams. Five chances to grow. Which one's dying fastest?"

[PANEL 3]
Shot Type & Angle: Close-up on notebook page.
Visual Description: Jessica's notebook showing a hand-drawn diagram of a hockey rink in the center with five arrows pointing inward. Each arrow is clearly labeled: "1. TICKETS", "2. MEDIA", "3. SPONSORS", "4. MERCHANDISE", "5. PREMIUM". Next to TICKETS she has written "PROBLEM #1" underlined twice.
Dialogue (JESSICA): "In Toronto, the Leafs sell out every night at $400 average. Nashville? $85 average. 3,000 empty seats per game."

[PAGE 4]
Layout Note: 4 panels in clockwise arrangement.
Style Note: Investigation montage with varied lighting.

[PANEL 1]
Shot Type & Angle: Wide shot of empty arena.
Visual Description: Jessica walks through empty arena seats with MARCUS, a young Black man holding a tablet. Thousands of empty seats stretch around them.
Dialogue (MARCUS): "Lower bowl is usually 80% full. Upper deck... maybe 50% on a good night."
Dialogue (JESSICA): "What about season tickets?"
Dialogue (MARCUS): "We're at 11,000. League average is 14,000."

[PANEL 2]
Shot Type & Angle: Medium shot in luxury suite.
Visual Description: Jessica stands in a dated, dusty luxury suite with old furniture and a small TV. Through the window, the pristine ice gleams below.
Dialogue (JESSICA): "When was this last renovated?"
Dialogue (VOICE): "2008, ma'am."
Dialogue (JESSICA): "And we're charging premium prices for... this?"
Caption: STREAM 1 - TICKETS. Problem identified.

[PANEL 3]
Shot Type & Angle: Medium shot in broadcast booth.
Visual Description: Jessica sits in the TV broadcast booth with WAYNE, a grizzled veteran in a headset. Monitors show camera angles. Charts show declining viewership.
Dialogue (WAYNE): "Regional sports deal pays us $15 million. Leafs get $60 million."
Dialogue (JESSICA): "What about streaming?"
Dialogue (WAYNE): "Nashville's a football town. Hockey's still proving itself."
Caption: STREAM 2 - MEDIA. The long game.

[PANEL 4]
Shot Type & Angle: Wide shot at loading dock.
Visual Description: Jessica walks through a team store loading dock with DENISE, a sharp Black woman in her 50s. Boxes of unsold jerseys are stacked everywhere.
Dialogue (DENISE): "We've got $2 million in dead inventory. Wrong player, wrong size."
Dialogue (JESSICA): "What sells best?"
Dialogue (DENISE): "Anything with just the logo."
Caption: STREAM 4 - MERCHANDISE. Stop the bleeding.

[PANEL 3]

Dialogue (JESSICA): "Five streams. All of them trickling. But they don't have to be."
Caption: She had three years to turn the franchise around.
