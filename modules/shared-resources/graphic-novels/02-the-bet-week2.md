# The $5.2 Billion Bet: A Rogers Media Graphic Novel Script
**Week 2: Media, Content & Fan Engagement**
**Style:** Retro Sports Illustrated - 1970s-80s painted sports magazine look with warm colors and visible brushstrokes
**Purpose:** Introduce media rights valuation, streaming economics, and the strategic calculus behind mega-deals
