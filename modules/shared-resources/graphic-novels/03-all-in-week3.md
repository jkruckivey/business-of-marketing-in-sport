# All In: A Sports Betting Graphic Novel Script
**Week 3: Sponsorship & Betting**
**Style:** Retro Sports Illustrated - 1970s-80s painted sports magazine look with warm colors and visible brushstrokes
**Purpose:** Introduce sponsorship activation, betting partnerships, and the transformation of fan engagement through legalized sports gambling

---

[COVER PAGE]
Title: ALL IN
Visual Description: Painted illustration of a Black woman in her 40s (DENISE CARTER) standing confidently in front of a massive digital sportsbook display showing live odds. She wears a sharp blazer with a subtle FanDuel pin. Behind her, a packed stadium roars with fans, some checking their phones. Green money and golden light swirl around her. Playing cards, dice, and sports equipment float in the background. The feeling is electric, modern, and high-stakes. 16:9 aspect ratio.

Caption: What if fans didn't just watch the game... what if they had something at stake?
Dialogue (DENISE): "I'm here about the partnerships role. I have some ideas about what comes next."

Dialogue (COUNSELOR): "Most companies see us as critics. You're the first to ask how we can work together."
Dialogue (DENISE): "The industry survives only if people trust it. That means getting this right."
