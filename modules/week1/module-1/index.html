Module Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

1

Analyze Regional Sports Media Rights

Evaluate how regional sports properties like the NHL Canucks determine media rights value and negotiate distribution partnerships in the Canadian market.

2

Assess Regional Brand Strategy

Examine how regional sports teams balance local fan engagement with broader media visibility through content and distribution decisions.

3

Evaluate Distribution Channel Trade-offs

Compare regional broadcasting partnerships with national platforms and assess the strategic implications for team marketing.

4

Apply Media Strategy Insights

Use case analysis and executive insights to develop strategic recommendations for regional sports properties.

Week 1 Case Study

NHL Vancouver Canucks: Competing on Revenue Sharing (W14149)

Examine how the Vancouver Canucks navigated media rights, regional broadcasting partnerships, and fan engagement in a changing media landscape.

Case Context:

  • Regional Sports Brand Strategy: How the Canucks position themselves in the Canadian market
  • Media Rights Negotiations: Distribution partnerships and revenue sharing models
  • Local vs. National Visibility: Balancing regional identity with broader reach
  • Fan Engagement: Connecting with audiences across traditional and digital platforms

Executive Leadership Session

Bell Media / TSN / RDS Executive

Connecting Industry Practice to Case Study: Following your analysis of the Vancouver Canucks case, hear directly from a senior executive at Bell Media about:

  • Regional Sports Properties: How TSN approaches regional NHL team partnerships
  • Media Rights Strategy: Valuation and negotiation approaches for team-broadcaster relationships
  • Content Distribution: Balancing regional broadcast with national platforms
  • Fan Engagement: Using media partnerships to build and retain audiences

Preparation: Come ready to discuss your Canucks case insights and ask questions about applying these concepts in practice.

Accessibility Note: All live sessions include real-time captions, and recordings with full transcripts will be available within 24 hours. Session materials are provided in multiple formats (audio, visual, text) to support diverse learning preferences.
