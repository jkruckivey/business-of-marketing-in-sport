Why This Matters

🏒 Canadian Context

Rogers paid $5.2 billion for NHL rights. TSN and RDS dominate Canadian sports broadcasting. Understanding these deals is crucial for any sports marketing career in Canada.

📺 Global Trends

Sports rights are the last "appointment television." While other content moves to on-demand, live sports drive massive audiences and subscription decisions.

💡 Career Applications

Whether you work for leagues, teams, agencies, or brands, understanding media valuations and content strategy is essential for strategic decision-making.
