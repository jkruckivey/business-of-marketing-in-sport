What This Reveals

  • Diversification wins. The balanced portfolio outperforms all focused strategies by 5-18% over 5 years.
  • Caps kill concentration. Ticketing-heavy hits the $30M capacity ceiling by Year 4. Media-heavy hits the $80M contract cap. Growth stops while diversified strategies keep compounding.
  • High growth ≠ best strategy. Betting grows 35%/year but starts small ($5M). Even at 50% focus, it only reaches $23M—less than media at base growth.
  • Risk matters. Betting-heavy has highest volatility. One regulatory change could devastate the portfolio.
  • Real sports teams diversify. This is why leagues invest in media deals, premium seating, sponsorship activation, and betting partnerships simultaneously.
