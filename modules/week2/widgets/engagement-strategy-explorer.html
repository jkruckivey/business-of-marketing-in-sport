Betting-Integrated Strategy

Base Fan Value $10.0M
Betting Partnerships (10% bet, +$30/fan) +$3.0M
Fantasy Integration (15% play, +$15/fan) +$2.25M
Investment Cost (revenue share model) -$0.5M
Net Annual Gain +$4.0M
Analysis: Betting partnerships work on revenue share, meaning the sportsbook pays YOU for access to your fans. Fantasy has minimal costs. This explains why ESPN, FOX, and others pay premium prices for rights when they have betting integration.
