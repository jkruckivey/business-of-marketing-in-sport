NHL Rights Bidding Simulator

Key insight: Split packages generate more total revenue than exclusive deals

Your Role: NHL Commissioner

Canada's NHL rights are expiring. Three companies want them. Do you take the highest exclusive bid, or split rights among multiple partners?

Rogers
$450M/yr
Regional rights only. Cautious after 2014 deal losses.
DAZN
$550M/yr
Aggressive growth. Needs NHL as anchor content.
Apple TV+
$700M/yr
Strategic acquisition. Global ambitions, deep pockets.
Option 1: Exclusive Deal
$700M/yr
Apple TV+ gets all rights (national, regional, streaming, international)
  • Simple, single partner
  • Apple's global reach
  • Fans need Apple TV+
  • Lower total revenue
Option 2: Split Package
$1.15B/yr
Rogers $450M (regional) + DAZN $400M (national) + Apple $300M (streaming)
  • 64% more revenue
  • Better fan access
  • Complex (3 partners)
  • Coordination needed
