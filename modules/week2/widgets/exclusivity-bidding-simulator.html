Your Decision: Exclusive or Split?

Select one option to see the trade-offs

Option 1: Exclusive Deal
$700M/year
Apple TV+ gets all rights (national, regional, streaming, international)

Pros:

  • Predictable, single-partner revenue
  • Simple contract management
  • Apple's global reach expands NHL brand
  • Highest per-partner value

Cons:

  • Canadian fans need Apple TV+ subscription ($15/month)
  • Lower total revenue vs. split package
  • Reduced accessibility (paywall)
  • Single point of failure if Apple strategy changes
Option 2: Split Package
$1.15B/year
Rogers $450M (regional) + DAZN $400M (national) + Apple $300M (streaming/international)

Pros:

  • 64% higher total revenue ($1.15B vs. $700M)
  • Better fan accessibility (multiple platforms)
  • Risk diversification across partners
  • Maximizes competitive bidding

Cons:

  • Complex coordination (3 partners)
  • Potential scheduling conflicts
  • More contracts to manage
  • Risk of disputes between partners
