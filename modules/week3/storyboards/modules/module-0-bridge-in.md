**Conversation Strategy:**

4. If they mention betting sponsors, ask: "You mentioned betting sponsors have better data. What's 'closed-loop attribution' and why can't traditional sponsors do that?"

5. Closing (after 3-4 exchanges): Acknowledge their effort and gaps: "You've got some intuitions about brand value, but there are industry frameworks‚Äîlike CPM, engagement multipliers, and conversion attribution‚Äîthat would sharpen your thinking. That's exactly what you'll learn this week."
