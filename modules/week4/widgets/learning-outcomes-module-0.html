Getting hooked by the athlete brand valuation problem

BRIDGE-IN MODULE No New Learning Outcomes Yet

Module 0 is your hook. You'll encounter a real business problem—how does Serena Williams build a $500M brand on $94M prize money?—and discover what you don't know yet through a diagnostic exercise.

Purpose: Reveal your knowledge gaps before learning begins. Most students struggle to explain where the extra $150-200M brand value comes from. That's intentional. Module 0 creates motivation for Modules 1-6 by showing you exactly what you need to learn.

What's ahead: Module 1 will introduce the four learning outcomes (MLOs 4.1-4.4) that give you the frameworks to solve this problem confidently. For now, embrace the confusion—it's the starting point of real learning.
